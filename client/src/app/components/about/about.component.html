About

Choordify is a platform designed to enhance your Spotify experience, by providing two main functionalities:

  1. Playlist organization: Easily optimize your playlists by rearranging tracks to create a seamless listening journey.
  2. Song recommendations: Ever faced the dilemma of where to add a new favorite song? Choordify analyzes each track's unique features and suggests the most compatible playlists, ensuring your music library stays finely tuned.

Whether you're fine-tuning your playlists or discovering new music, Choordify utilizes seven different song attributes to provide tailored recommendations and improve your listening experience.
